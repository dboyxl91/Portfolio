Portfolio Research

Portfolios are very important!

Portfolios are important in regards to my intended career because I am graduating with a bachlors in I.T. which is a broad degree so the portfolio would showcase my knowledge and in what I do best. A portfolio shows what you do best in a clear and concise way.

What do you need to include?

In your portfolio you should have a number of things documented. The first part of your portfolio should include a summary of accomplishments, skills, and past experience. The next part of your portfolio you should include what you do in your career, how you act in a professional enviroment, and what you bring that others don't. You should also always include a section about yourself and the story of what brought you to where you are today.
